I'm Isaiah, a third year mathematics PhD student at the University of Nebraska – Lincoln advised by Mark Walker.
Broadly speaking, my research interests lie in homological and derived category methods in commutative algebra.



Before UNL, I was a BAMM! scholar at Fresno State, where I worked on Khovanov homology (an algebraic knot invariant) under the supervision of Carmen Caprau.
